According to the MIT paper, "Moving Learning Games Forward," games in schools today can be used as...

Authoring Platforms: Game is used to produce and artifact, be it another game, a model, visual text, or written text.

Ex: Students produce a model in StarCraft.

Ex: Students gain knowlege of Carribean History by playing Pirates.

Content Systems Games deliver content about a particular subject area.

Simulations: Sudents use games to test theories about systems and tinker with variables.

Ex: Students gain a systematic understanding of engineering problems by working with a limited budet and available materials in Bridge Builder.

Ex: Dundeons & Daragons is used to explore probability.

Trigger Systems Games are used as a jumping point for discussion.
